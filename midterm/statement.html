Design Statement

I purposely made the site very simples so users could feel calm and not overwhelmed by an overly styled website. I also put the same menu bar on every page so the website is clear and straightforward to vistors, but also gave each page a different japanese textured background to create some diffrentiation. I also added the japanese patterns to give a feeling related to tokyo.

I chose the text, images, and the style of the buttons to be simple and rounded to create a minimalistic and friendly look. I also made the website mostly left aligned because in design, the eye is mostly drawn to the left then to the right. I put buttons on the top of the page and under activities and dining options as directories to booking and an easy way to navigate throughout the website.
