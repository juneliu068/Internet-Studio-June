Guests of Honor

Hiccup of Berk

Hiccup shows how empathy and courage can reshape a whole culture. I’d love to ask how he learned to lead with understanding, and what it took to bridge fear and friendship between Vikings and dragons.

Poppy from Trolls

Poppy’s optimism is contagious. I’d invite her to learn how she keeps joy alive during tough times, and how music and community help her bring people together.

Hawk Moth from ladybug

Hawk Moth would bring the sharp edge to the discussion. I’d ask why he believes the ends justify the means, and whether understanding others’ pain could ever change his choices.
