Six Thinking Hats

Overview

We’re building a UTD only marketplace where students can buy and sell furniture, school supplies, and dorm essentials. It focuses on safety and convenience: @utdallas.edu login, in-app chat, and suggested public meet-up spots on campus for quick pickups. The goal is to save students money and time, reduce waste through reuse, and make trading feel trustworthy. We’ll start with a small simple version, measure listings and completed trades, and improve based on student feedback.

White Hat

Our users are UTD students buying and selling furniture and school supplies. We need to know what items they want most, when they shop, and how they like to pay. We also need safe meet-up spots on campus and what fees feel fair. We can learn this with a short survey and a small pilot.

Red Hat

Students feel excited to find cheap, nearby items. They also worry about scams and no-shows. Simple steps and clear safety tips will help them feel calm and confident.

Black Hat

Scams, spam posts, and low inventory can hurt trust. Unsafe or confusing meet ups are a risk. Disputes over price or payment may happen and will need rules.

Yellow Hat

A UTD only market is close, fast, and affordable. It saves money and time, and it reduces waste by reusing items. It also helps students help each other.

Green Hat

Use @utdallas.edu login and in app chat only. Show suggested public meet-up spots on campus. Add move-in/move-out specials, textbook swaps, wishlists, and price tips. Start with no listing fees to get more posts.

Blue Hat

Goal: prove students want this and can trade safely. Launch a simple MVP with login, reporting, and meet up suggestions first. Track listings, matches, completed trades, and reports. Improve every two weeks based on what we see.
