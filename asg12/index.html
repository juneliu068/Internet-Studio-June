12 of My Favorite Movies

These are some of my favorite movies because they all mix heart, humor, and imagination in different ways. I love stories about unlikely heroes, whether it’s a rat who wants to be a chef, shy girls who discover secret worlds, or ordinary people caught up in something bigger than themselves. Many of these films are beautifully animated with magical creatures and whimsical worlds that make me feel like a kid again, while others explore faith, friendship, and love in a sincere way. Together they remind me to be brave, follow my calling, and believe that kindness and hope can change everything.

Ratatoullie

A shy rat follows his dream of becoming a great French chef.

Barbie and the 12 Dancing Princesses

Twelve sisters discover a magical world where they can secretly dance at night.

How to Train Your Dragon

A Viking boy befriends a dragon and changes his village’s view of dragons.

Jesus Revolution

A pastor and a group of young hippies spark a spiritual revival in the 1970s.

Wicked

The story shows how the “Wicked Witch” of Oz became misunderstood instead of truly evil.

Kpop Demon Hunters

A K-pop girl group battles demons while trying to keep their music careers.

The Emperor's New Groove

A selfish emperor turned into a llama must learn humility to regain his throne.

Nezha

A boy born with great power fights his destiny and chooses to protect his people.

Ponyo

A goldfish girl becomes human and brings magical chaos to a small seaside town.

Howl's Moving Castle

A cursed girl and a mysterious wizard travel together in a magical moving castle.

Totoro

Two sisters meet gentle forest spirits who help them through a difficult time.

27 Dresses

A woman who has been a bridesmaid 27 times finally faces her own love story.
